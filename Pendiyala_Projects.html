The reader ahs plenty of options to choose from, each leading them down a different path. My role in this project was mainly to focus on the coding, as well as contributing ideas for our game. My partner, Siri, focused on the documentation of our project (Click here), which explained every decesion that we made to get to our final game. Although there were many probles that we encountered throughut our project, Siri and I used teamwork to solve them. Siri and I used teamwork to solve this problem, as she handed out suggestions for what might be wrong, while I fixed the code. We soon found our problem and fixed it effortlessly because of our teamwork and resiliance
